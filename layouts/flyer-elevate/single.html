{{ i18n "What upgrade paths are supported?" }}

{{ i18n "CentOS 6 → CentOS 7 → AlmaLinux 8 or CentOS Stream 8. Then 8 → 9 → 10 within AlmaLinux or CentOS Stream. LEAPP now supports CentOS Stream and AlmaLinux natively upstream." }}

{{ i18n "Will migration be in-place?" }}

{{ i18n "Yes. All your data, applications and settings will be kept. Migrations will require your system to reboot twice." }}

{{ i18n "Why include CentOS 6?" }}

{{ i18n "We know how hard sysadmins work because most of us have done it! Adding CentOS 6 to ELevate has already improved the lives of sysadmins who couldn't migrate applications between devices." }}
