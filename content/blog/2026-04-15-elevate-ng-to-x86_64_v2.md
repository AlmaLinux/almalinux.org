ELevate NG (the development branch of ELevate) now supports the upgrade path from AlmaLinux OS 9 to AlmaLinux OS 10 (or AlmaLinux OS Kitten) x86_64_v2. We need your help to verify that the upgrade works smoothly. Please give it a try and report your results!

**Caution: Do not use it on production machines.** We highly recommend making backups and/or snapshots of your system before proceeding, and performing a trial run in a sandbox first.

## How to upgrade

Follow the [Upgrading AlmaLinux 9 to AlmaLinux 10 x86_64_v2](https://wiki.almalinux.org/elevate/ELevate-NG-testing-guide.html#upgrading-almalinux-9-to-almalinux-10-x86-64-v2) instructions on the AlmaLinux Wiki.

Note that this upgrade path uses a new method of target userspace bootstrapping with an x86_64_v2 rootfs image that you download as part of the process.
