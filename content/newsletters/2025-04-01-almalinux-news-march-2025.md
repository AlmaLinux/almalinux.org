## Quick Notes

- **Security Patch Released**: AlmaLinux published patches addressing the Freetype Security vulnerability CVE-2025-27363. Details are available on the official blog.

- **Japan User Group**: The AlmaLinux Japan User Group invites community members to connect via Mattermost chat and attend regular meetups through their Connpass page.

## Ways to Contribute

**Translation Volunteers Needed**: The AlmaLinux website has surpassed 80% translation completion in five languages, with three more approaching that milestone. Contributors can join via Weblate to help expand accessibility.

**Forum Support**: Community members are encouraged to answer questions on AlmaLinux forums, assisting fellow sysadmins, developers, and DevOps professionals.

## Blog Highlights

**FreeType Vulnerability**: Meta disclosed a critical flaw in FreeType versions 2.13.0 and below enabling remote code execution. The blog post details the vulnerability and invites users to test production patches.

**CloudFest Attendance**: AlmaLinux representatives participated in CloudFest, with coverage available on their blog.

**ALESCo Updates**: The AlmaLinux Engineering Steering Committee reported progress on security responses and community RFC requests, offering opportunities for community involvement.
