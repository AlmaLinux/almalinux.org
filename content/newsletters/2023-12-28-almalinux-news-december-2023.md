Hello everyone! Welcome to this month's AlmaLinux newsletter, where we share the latest AlmaLinux happenings, upcoming events, and more!

## Quick Notes

- AlmaLinux 8 GPG Key updates on January 12th — action required
- New board members welcomed
- 2023 recap available
- EPEL support added to ELevate
- Cloud and Live Media images updated for 8.9 and 9.3
- FOSDEM 2024 in Brussels (February 3-4)

## Action Required: AlmaLinux 8 GPG Key Change

Due to storage issues with the original master key, AlmaLinux 8 is receiving a key update effective January 12th. Users should review the blog post for preparation steps.

## Board Expansion

We've expanded the AlmaLinux OS Foundation's board by two seats, adding Alex Iribarren from CERN, and Jun Yoshida from Cybertrust.

## ELevate EPEL Support

The first step in EPEL support is now included in the ELevate project, enabling smooth migrations between CentOS 7 and AlmaLinux 8. Current limitations exclude non-AlmaLinux OS migrations and EL8-to-EL9 transitions.

## Image Updates

Cloud, container, and live media images have been updated for versions 8.9 and 9.3, featuring newly available UEFI boot support.

## FOSDEM 2024

AlmaLinux will host the Distributions Dev Room and operate a booth at FOSDEM 2024 in Brussels on February 3-4. Volunteers interested in staffing events should contact the Marketing SIG.

## Stay Connected

Join the Newsletters mailing list or connect via LinkedIn for updates. The AlmaLinux Foundation welcomes new members.
