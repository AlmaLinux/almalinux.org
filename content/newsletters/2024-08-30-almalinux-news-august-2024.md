## Welcome & Quick Notes

The newsletter opens with several key announcements:

- **Seeking contributors** for various AlmaLinux initiatives
- **ELevate received a major update** with expanded capabilities
- **Stevie Awards nomination**: AlmaLinux is nominated for The Stevie® Awards' People's Choice award in the Government or Non-profit category

## Call for Help

**Swag Shop Manager Needed**: The Marketing SIG is recruiting someone to audit inventory, evaluate costs, update offerings, and develop fresh designs. Interested parties should contact benny at almalinux.org.

**Forum Support**: Forum traffic has nearly doubled in six months. Volunteers are needed to answer questions from sysadmins, developers, and DevOps engineers at lnx.rocks/forumhelp.

## Blog Highlights

**Summer Events Recap**: AlmaLinux attended Southeast Linux Fest, Academy Open Source days, SIGGRAPH, and Flock to Fedora.

**Mirror System Growth**: Since August 2021, the mirror network expanded from approximately 100 servers to over 400, supporting over one million systems worldwide with geo-location features.

**ELevate Update**: The largest update since 2021 promotes ELevateNG to stable status, now supporting CentOS Stream 8-9 upgrades, Scientific Linux migrations, and improved hardware detection.
