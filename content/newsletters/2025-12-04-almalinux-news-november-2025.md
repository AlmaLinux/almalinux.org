## Key Announcements

**Two Major Releases:** AlmaLinux 9.7 ("Moss Jungle Cat") and AlmaLinux 10.1 ("Heliotrope Lion") have launched. Version 10.1 introduces Btrfs support while maintaining RHEL 10.1 compatibility.

**New Special Interest Group:** The Media & Entertainment SIG focuses on supporting visual effects, animation, and post-production professionals using AlmaLinux.

**Upcoming Event:** Japan's AlmaLinux Users Group will host their third AlmaLinux Day in Tokyo during 2026.
