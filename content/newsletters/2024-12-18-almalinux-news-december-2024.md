Hi there! Welcome to this month's AlmaLinux newsletter! We know that a lot of us are headed out for an extended break, so we're sending the December newsletter out a bit early — and that means it's a bit shorter than normal!

## Quick Notes

- AlmaLinux 10 beta is now available
- Planning for the 2025 event schedule is underway

## Looking for Ways to Help?

**Help on the forums**

Traffic to the AlmaLinux forums has been steadily increasing, and having answers to questions there is more important than ever. Use the link [lnx.rocks/forumhelp](https://lnx.rocks/forumhelp) to help your fellow sysadmins, developers, and DevOps engineers with opinions, answers, and feedback.

**Volunteer for the Distros Room at FOSDEM**

The Linux Distributions room at FOSDEM will be on Sunday, February 2nd, and volunteers are needed. Whether you want to help staff the door, announce speakers, or help speakers stay on time, there's plenty to take care of. Sign up for the FOSDEM Distributions Devroom mailing list to receive the volunteer sheet.

## Latest from the AlmaLinux Blog

**AlmaLinux 10.0 Beta Now Available**

AlmaLinux 10.0 Beta "Purple Lion" is now available for all supported architectures. This is a BETA release and should not be used for production installations. AlmaLinux OS Kitten is designed for development and preparation purposes; the Beta release is the next step toward stable.

**Announcing AlmaLinux Day: Tokyo 2024**

AlmaLinux Day: Tokyo 2024 took place this month. A post-event blog post and session recordings are available on the AlmaLinux blog and YouTube channel.

**Volunteering at Nerdearla in Buenos Aires with AlmaLinux**

One of the AlmaLinux volunteer staffers at the Nerdearla event in October shared their story of volunteering at the event on the AlmaLinux blog.

## Upcoming Events

Big plans are in the works for 2025! If you enjoy chatting with folks about Linux and open source and are interested in helping staff a booth at an event, fill out the volunteer Google form. Travel and hotel are covered for any events attended on behalf of AlmaLinux.
