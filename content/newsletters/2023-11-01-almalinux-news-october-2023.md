Hello everyone! Welcome to this month's AlmaLinux newsletter, where we share the latest AlmaLinux happenings, upcoming events, and more!

## Quick Notes

- The Foundation is holding an election to fill three seats on the AlmaLinux OS Foundation board; non-members should apply for membership soon to be eligible to vote
- AlmaLinux 8.9 and 9.3 betas will be released soon
- Recent improvements have been made to the mirror system; mirror operators should review their traffic and reach out with questions

## Latest from the AlmaLinux Blog

**AlmaLinux OS Foundation Election - 2023**

Three seats on the AlmaLinux OS Foundation board are open for election. Community members can nominate candidates or vote. Membership applications must be submitted soon to participate in the vote.

**AlmaLinux for Data Science, Containers, and ARM**

The College of Letters and Science at the University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB) uses AlmaLinux in day-to-day operations, including as the build infrastructure behind data science environments serving hundreds to thousands of students per term.

**FIPS Validation for AlmaLinux OS**

AlmaLinux 9.2 has achieved FIPS compliance, thanks to CloudLinux. FIPS 140-3 will be valid for anyone using AlmaLinux OS 9.2 for as long as it is supported.
