Hello everyone! Welcome to this month's AlmaLinux newsletter, where we share the latest AlmaLinux happenings, upcoming events, and more! A lot is happening already, so make sure you take a look below.

## Quick Notes

- If you're at CentOS Connect, don't miss Elkhan Mammadli's talk today
- Attending FOSDEM? Catch benny at the panel session on Saturday, and Andrew Lukoshko's talk on Sunday
- We're hosting a CloudFest Hackathon project to make OS upgrades easier
- We've made some updates to our ELevate project! You'll see it in the blog highlights below, or you can read the full blog post
- The session recordings for AlmaLinux Day: Tokyo are now available
- We've also announced AlmaLinux Day: Germany (adjacent to CloudFest), and we hope to see you there on March 18th!

## Latest from the AlmaLinux Blog

**AlmaLinux Day: Tokyo recap, announcing AlmaLinux Day: Germany, and more!**

Last December we hosted the very first AlmaLinux Day in Tokyo, and the session replays are now available. You can check them out on our YouTube channel. We're also planning and attending a number of other events this year, including AlmaLinux Day: Germany.

**ELevate Project Updates**

We've made a number of updates to our ELevate project recently. In addition to RHEL and EPEL, ELevate now offers 3rd party repository support. We also launched a new version of ELevate, ELevate Next Generation (NG).

**Q&A Videos + How to Get Involved**

To address some of the common community questions, we are creating short Q&A videos that will be shared on outreach platforms. The blog post explains how you can get involved!

**AlmaLinux Cloud, Container and Live Media Images Updates**

AlmaLinux OS provides a variety of cloud images. Images have been updated to 9.3 and 8.9 versions. All Live Media images were also updated to versions AlmaLinux 9.3 and AlmaLinux 8.9.

## Stay Up to Date and Join Us!

If you want to make sure you catch every update, sign up for the Newsletters mailing list or subscribe on LinkedIn. You can also join the AlmaLinux OS Foundation if you haven't already.

Thank you for such a great start to the year!
