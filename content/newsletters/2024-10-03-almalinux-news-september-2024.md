## Introduction

Benny Vasquez, Chair of the AlmaLinux OS Foundation Board, shares September updates slightly delayed, highlighting new initiatives and community opportunities.

## Key Announcements

**Certification SIG Launch**

The organization unveiled a new Certification Special Interest Group alongside a hardware certification program, offering immediate benefits and multiple involvement opportunities.

**Bootc Images Available**

AlmaLinux now provides bootc images for Intel/AMD (x86_64) and ARM64 architectures, supporting a new deployment method used by RHEL's Image mode and HeliumOS.

**Board Member Video Series**

A new video series introduces AlmaLinux OS Foundation board members to the community, with initial features on Jack Aboutbol and Jun Yoshida.

**ELevate Project Updates**

Recent improvements were released; details available in the changelog and dedicated blog post.

## Community Opportunities
