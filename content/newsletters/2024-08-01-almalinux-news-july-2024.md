## Key Highlights

**Security Updates**

Three critical security patches were released for AlmaLinux OS 9:

- CVE-2024-6387 (regreSSHion) - OpenSSH vulnerability patched same-day on July 1st
- CVE-2024-6409 - Signal handler race condition in OpenSSH addressed
- The team proactively built these updates independently, with support from the newly-formed ALESCo technical steering committee

**Sponsorship Announcements**

Three new Silver Sponsor Members joined for 2024: Codenotary, ATIX AG, and StorPool Storage AD. Additionally, Cybertrust Japan renewed their platinum sponsorship, strengthening AlmaLinux's presence in Asia.

**Technical Development**

- Testing needed for a glibc regression affecting VFX community software on Enterprise Linux 9.4
- ELevateNG (Next Generation) scheduled to move from testing to stable release the week of August 5th
- Significant automation improvements made to cloud, container, and live media image creation

**CentOS 7 Transition Support**

Following CentOS 7's end-of-life on June 30th, AlmaLinux provided migration guidance for users seeking upgrade alternatives to CentOS Stream.
