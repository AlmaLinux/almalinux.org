## Key Highlights

- **Raspberry Pi 5 Support**: AlmaLinux now supports Raspberry Pi 5, expanding hardware compatibility across multiple Pi models
- **New Sponsors**: Three additional silver sponsors announced, growing the foundation's backing
- **ELevate Upgrades**: Critical tool for migrating CentOS 6 and 7 systems featured in user success story

## Blog Updates

**How ELevate Supports Business Needs**

David der Nederlanden shares insights on how AlmaLinux and ELevate address enterprise requirements, featuring practical guidance from real-world migration experiences.

**2024 Sponsors Announcement**

The foundation recognized multiple companies contributing to open source through sponsorship, showcasing growing industry support.

## Raspberry Pi Expansion

AlmaLinux now officially supports:

- Raspberry Pi 5 (since June 2024 images)
- Raspberry Pi 4 Model B
- Raspberry Pi 400
- Raspberry Pi 3 Model B+
- Raspberry Pi 3 Model A+
