Hello everyone! Welcome to this month's AlmaLinux newsletter, where we share the latest AlmaLinux happenings, upcoming events, and more!

## Quick Notes

- We'll be at SCaLE March 14-17! Swing by our booth to see us.
- We're hosting a CloudFest Hackathon project March 16-17, and you can also catch us at CloudFest March 18-22!
- In addition, we're hosting AlmaLinux Day: Germany adjacent to CloudFest, and we hope to see you there on March 18th!
- We hit three big events in February: CentOS Connect, FOSDEM, and the AlmaLinux Leadership Summit. Read all about them on the blog!
- We're looking for volunteers from the AlmaLinux community to help staff our booths. If you can dedicate at least 1/2 of the event to staffing a booth at any of the events below, fill out the Google form! We'll cover your travel and hotel, and you'll get a per-diem to help offset your food and assorted travel-related costs.
