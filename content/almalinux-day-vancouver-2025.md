    <h1>Where nature and tech come together</h1>
    <div>
      AlmaLinux Day: Vancouver brings together artists, developers, and sysadmins working at the intersection of visual effects and open-source systems — with a special spotlight on AlmaLinux as a stable, community-driven platform powering modern production workflows. In the heart of Vancouver's cinematic and rich landscape, and hosted in the perfect spot, this conference is a space to connect, share, and build the future of digital creativity.
    </div>
